Theory¶

This section will describe the research behind this project and how it would benefit the lives of your community.

Features¶

  1. 4x PV Solar Panels on the roof

  2. Electrical charge controller, batteries, inverter, etc

  3. Large Individual storage lockers for volunteers” personal belongings

  4. Ample shelf-space for tools

  5. Two large covered outdoor spaces for living & working

Concept¶

Typically if someone buys land somewhere remote (off-grid & without utilities), they first bring-in diesel generators to power the tools needed to begin construction.

The «Genesis Booth» is an alternative that would permit constructing a new off-grid community or homestead using renewable, solar energy.

The expectation is that people move-in with camping gear. They purchase four 1 meter x 2 meter solar panels and spend their first weeks building the Genesis Booth. After the booth is complete, they have a weather-proof shed for storing tools, enough power for metal- and wood-working tools (saws, drills, welder) and a covered workspace to continue construction (eg the Eco-Libre Launch Nest).
